Your Ultimate PC Building Guide

Key Components

If you're opting for high-performance PCs, be prepared to invest some serious cash. As always, your choices must include:

  • CPU
  • GPU
  • Motherboard
CPU, GPU, and Motherboard

For the CPU, consider AMD Ryzen 9 7900X3D or Intel i7-13700K. Both are powerhouses, perfect companions for the RTX 4070.

As for the GPU, the RTX 4070 is a gem, delivering 4K gaming prowess and more. Match it with a compatible DDR5 motherboard.

Additional Components

Additional components you'll need include PSU, case, RAM, CPU cooler or AIO, and additional fans depending on the case:

  • PSU: Opt for 1000W or higher.
  • Case: Choose a spacious, airflow-focused option like the NZXT H7 Elite.
  • RAM: A minimum of 32GB at 3600Hz or faster.
  • AIO Cooler: Enhance aesthetics and cooling with a 2-3 fan AIO of around 320mm.
  • Fans: Aim for a setup with 4 intake and 3 exhaust fans, potentially more for optimal airflow.
